Game Rules

There are l lines in the NxM board. The players are putting figures one in each turn to the board. If the attacker can fill a line with his/her figures, he/she wins. On the other hand, if he cannot in the end, the defender wins.

The lines are costruated such that there is no 7-line in the space, that doesn't contain one of the l lines. This results that if the Defender can cover all the l lines in each ROWxCOL board, he/she covers all the 7-line in the space too.
